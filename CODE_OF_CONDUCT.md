# Code of Conduct

Factile contributors and maintainers are expected to keep project spaces
professional, direct, and useful.

Expected behavior:

- Be respectful when disagreeing.
- Focus criticism on code, behavior, and project decisions.
- Assume public comments may be read by new contributors.
- Respect maintainer decisions about project scope and timing.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, threats, sustained disruption,
personal attacks, or publishing private information without permission.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, restrict participation, or take
other reasonable action to protect the project.
